## Solution

### Ex 2/18

1. The initial unit logs the moment when every vehicle crosses its path.
2. It utilizes OCR software to recognize and record the vehicle's number plate for identification.
3. Subsequently, this data is transmitted to the second unit.
4. The second unit similarly registers the time of passage for each vehicle.
5. The microprocessor computes the duration of travel between these two units.
6. Information regarding vehicles exceeding the speed limit is sent to the police headquarters, including their registration numbers.
7. A computer then cross-references each vehicle with the DVLC database.
8. It generates a letter addressed to the vehicle owner using mail merge for printing.
