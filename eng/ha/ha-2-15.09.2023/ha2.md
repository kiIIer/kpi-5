## Solution

### Ex. 6

1. What did you do today?
2. I worked on my project. I searched the Web for sites on digital cameras
3. Did you find any good ones?
4. I found several company sites - Sony, Canon, ... but I wanted one which compared all the models
5. Which search engine did you use?
6. Dogpile mostly. Have you ever used it?(should've used Google)
7. Yes, I've tried it but I've had more luck with Ask Jeeves. Why don't you try it?(Why don't you try GOOGLE?)
8. I've had enough for one night. I've spent hours on that project
9. I haven't started on mine yet
10. Yeh? I bet you've done it all.
