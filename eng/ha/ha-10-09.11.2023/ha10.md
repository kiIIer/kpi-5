## Solution

### Video

The operations of most stores are fairly uniform, involving a process where customers register through a mobile application. Upon entering, individuals are at liberty to select items of their choice, scanning them either with their personal devices or at designated checkout zones. Subsequently, a cashless transaction is conducted, allowing the purchasers to depart with their acquisitions. Nonetheless, each establishment bears its distinct characteristics. The first store, aptly designated as a 'general-purpose store,' offers a standard array of merchandise without any particularly remarkable products and operates without on-site personnel. The second establishment, a caf√©, introduces an innovative ordering system through voice recognition, followed by the preparation of your order by staff. Payments are processed upon exit. The final store is characterized by its mobility, equipped with wheels and operated remotely. Its design is futuristic, with the intent to eventually function autonomously. This nomadic store seeks out densely populated regions, thereby catering to areas with a high demand for retail services.

### Ex 63.1

1. She's going on holiday with some friends in August.
2. I'm sure he doesn't have any evidence for his accusations.
3. There was never any question that she would return home.
4. Wasn't there any problem about your tax last year? I remember you telling me about it.
5. It is reported that there has been some improvement in the President's condition.
6. There is seldom any world news in "The Daily Star."
7. I hope there wasn't any damage to your car.
8. Joan's mother scarcely ever let her have any friends round.
9. 'I'm going on holiday next week.' 'But haven't you got some important work to finish?'
10. There can hardly be any doubt that he is the best tennis player in the world.
